Introduction

Cloud computing is a significant breakthrough in the storage of data and industry technology. Through cloud computing information can be shared seemlessly across different applications and devices. Cloud computing is similar to public utility as it relies on sharing of resources to achieve coherence. Instead of storing your data on your hard drive or local server, Internet has made it possible to store and access your data and programs over the internet.

Cloud computing is generally defined in two ways. It is either based on the service that the cloud offers or it is based on the deployment model.
